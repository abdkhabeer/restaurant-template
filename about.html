Our Story

Founded in the heart of the city, Le Sel is a modern French restaurant where timeless technique meets bold, contemporary flavor. Our philosophy is simple: honor the craft, celebrate the season, and welcome every guest like family.

Chef Idris Moreau

With a background in Michelin-starred kitchens and a love for his grandmother’s recipes, Chef Idris brings heart and refinement to every dish. His menu is rooted in French tradition with modern flair, highlighting local ingredients and elegant presentation.
